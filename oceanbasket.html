OCEAN BASKET

Ocean Basket is South Africa’s favourite seafood restaurant, committed to giving you delicious seafood and that ‘home-away-from-home’ feeling. They offer an array of Mediterranean inspired dishes from prawns, sushi, and calamari to our famous fish and chips and their legendary creamy lemon sauce. With generous portions of quality seafood, Ocean Basket offers a delightful dining experience for you and your family.
