ABOUT PINECITY MALL

Pinecity is a shopping mall located in Waterfall City, Midrand, Gauteng, South Africa12. It is the largest single-phase shopping mall to be built in Africa, with a total retail area of 131 000 square metres and a gross area of 550 000 square metres2. The mall is home to over 300 shops, many of which are flagship stores, and boasts uniquely identified court areas made for easy shopping navigation
