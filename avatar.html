BRIEF SYPNOSIS

In the year 2154, Earth faces a severe energy crisis due to the depletion of natural resources. The Resources Development Administration (RDA) mines a valuable mineral called Unobtanium on Pandora, a densely forested habitable moon orbiting the fictional gas giant Polyphemus in the Alpha Centauri star system. However, Pandora’s atmosphere is poisonous to humans.

TOn Pandora, the Na’vi, a species of 10-foot tall, blue-skinned, sapient humanoids, live in harmony with nature and worship a mother goddess named Eywa. To explore this alien world, humans use Avatars—bio-engineered hybrids that allow them to link with Na’vi minds and move freely on Pandora.
