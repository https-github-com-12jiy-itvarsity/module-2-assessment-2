LITTLE MERMAID

AGE RESTRICTION

No Age restriction

CAST MEMBERS Halle Bailey will portray Ariel, the curious and adventurous mermaid who dreams of life on land. Jonah Hauer-King will play Prince Eric, the charming human prince who captures Ariel’s heart. Melissa McCarthy takes on the role of the villainous sea witch Ursula, known for her powerful magic and cunning ways. Javier Bardem portrays King Triton, Ariel’s father and ruler of the underwater kingdom

SHOWING TIMES The highly anticipated movie is coming to cinemas in South Africa on 26 May.

BRIEF SYPNOSIS

Ariel, a spirited young mermaid and the youngest daughter of King Triton, yearns for adventure beyond the sea. Despite her father’s prohibition on merfolk interacting with humans, Ariel secretly collects human artifacts and dreams of life above the waves. During a storm, she rescues the handsome Prince Eric and falls in love with him. Desperate to join Eric’s world, Ariel makes a fateful deal with the evil sea witch, Ursula, which allows her to experience life on land but puts her life and her father’s crown in jeopardy123.

The film explores Ariel’s journey as she follows her heart, defying the rules of her underwater kingdom, and risking everything for love and adventure..
